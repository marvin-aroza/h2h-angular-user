About Our Charity

We provide a platform for people who are suffering from the pandemic and to provide help by online medium. Join our team to help the needy.

The objective of this project is to implement a platform which will become mediator between helper and the people who is actually needs help and that help can be in any kind like food, clothes, shelter and medical awareness. If any person is having the capacity to help from their own with any direct and indirect way, they can help.
